Design Principles

Alignment

Squid Ink Restaurant

Squid Ink Restaurant

Squid Ink Restaurant uses a clean design with strong left alignment for it's web site navigation. It also uses a left alignment for the next column of information that is clear to your eye where to start reading.

White space

Pocket Penguins

Pocket Penguins Store

Pocket Penguins uses a lot of white space and few images to help you focus on what they feel is important.

Repetition

The Poem Farm

The Poem Farm

The Poem Farm not only demonstrates the use of repetition in their web page, but also explain to us other places to use repetition. They have a nice style with their labels with a solid color, font, and size so you know where articles start. They also encourage repetition in your written word such as books.
